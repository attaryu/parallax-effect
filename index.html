What is the parallax effect?

The Parallax effect is a visual effect which creates the illusion of movement towards or away from the user when they scroll down or up. This effect is usually applied in websites, blogs and other types of digital media where there is a need to display content in 3D space with realistic depth perception.

It plays an important role in the user experience of your website. It involved for you to become more attractive in the eyes of the user.
